How Can We Produce Food without Harming the Climate?

Our food system contributes roughly 30% of global greenhouse gas emissions. This guide tries to answer the following questions:


  1. Where do these emissions come from?
  2. What approaches could be taken to reduce them?
  3. How can I get involved in this transition?
